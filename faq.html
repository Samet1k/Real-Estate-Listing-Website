Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to buy real estate depends on the local market conditions, but generally, late fall and winter tend to have less competition.

To sell your property quickly, ensure it's priced competitively, presented well (clean and staged), and marketed effectively through online platforms.

Closing costs can include title insurance, inspection fees, attorney fees, and taxes. Typically, they range from 2% to 5% of the home price.

You can finance your first home through a mortgage. Consider different types of loans such as FHA, conventional, or VA loans, and choose the one that fits your financial situation.
